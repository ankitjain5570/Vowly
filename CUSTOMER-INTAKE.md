## 7. Sharing Different Invites to Different Guests (how it works)

You don't need to configure this — it's a feature you'll use after we hand over.
From your private **admin dashboard** you can:
- Pick exactly which events & sections a particular guest or group should see
  (e.g. "only Engagement + Wedding", or "Cocktail Night only").
- Generate a **secret link** for them. The link is scrambled, so guests can't tell
  which events they were (or weren't) invited to.
- Track all RSVPs — see who's coming, how many guests, and to which events, in a
  list or a drag-and-drop board.

Just let us know if you'd like a walkthrough of the dashboard.
